Account Deletion Guide

To delete your account from Money Account Together, please follow the instructions below.

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data and cannot be undone.

Data to be Deleted

  • Profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • All transaction records (income/expense records)
  • Category settings
  • Family connection information
  • Budget settings and statistics data
  • App settings and personalization information

How to Delete Your Account

1

Launch App and Sign In

Open the Money Account Together app and sign in with the account you want to delete.

2

Navigate to More Tab

Select the 'More' tab from the bottom menu.

3

Enter Settings Menu

Select the 'Settings' menu.

4

Select Account Management

Choose 'Account Management' from the menu.

5

Request Account Deletion

Tap the 'Delete Account' button and follow the prompts to proceed with deletion.

üí° Note: If you are the head of a family, you must transfer the head role to another member or dissolve the family before deleting your account.

Before Deleting Your Account

  • Back up any important transaction records.
  • Notify your family members about your account deletion.
  • Cancel any active subscriptions first.
  • You cannot re-register with the same email within 30 days after deletion.

Data Processing Policy

Upon account deletion request:

  • All personal data will be immediately deleted.
  • Data subject to legal retention requirements will be kept for the required period then deleted.
  • Anonymized statistical data may be retained for service improvement.
