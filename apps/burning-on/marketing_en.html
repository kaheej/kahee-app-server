About the App

Manage everything in one place: meal plans, weight, InBody data, water intake, fasting timer, progress photos, and personal notes. Reduce the hassle of searching for information and practice Switch On Diet more efficiently by checking all necessary data at a glance.

Based on Dr. Park Yong-woo's 33 years of clinical experience, this app supports both the scientifically proven 4-week program and intermittent fasting. Track your progress effortlessly with daily journals, providing a systematic and rewarding diet experience.

Key Features

Schedule Management

Automatically generate a 4-week program schedule and view it at a glance with the calendar view. Easily understand Phase-by-Phase meal changes and customize your own schedule.

Meal Management

Phase-specific meal plans are automatically provided, and you can easily track calories of foods you eat. Save frequently eaten foods and meal templates for quick entry, and register your own custom foods.

Intermittent Fasting Timer

Check fasting progress with real-time timer and receive notifications when you reach your target time. Fasting records are automatically saved and viewable in statistics.

Comprehensive Health Records

Record detailed InBody data including weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass, visceral fat, waist circumference. Manage all daily health data in one place: water intake, sleep hours, bowel movements, and personal notes.

Progress Photos

Take body photos and save them by date to visually track your body's changes over time.

Workout Records

Support 30+ workout types including walking, running, and strength training. Record running routes with GPS tracking, and automatically collect steps and stairs climbed data by integrating with HealthKit and Google Fit.

Statistics and Analysis

Visualize all data including weight, body fat, water intake, and workout records with graphs and charts to understand progress at a glance. Check statistics for desired periods with various filters: 7 days, 30 days, entire program, or custom date range.
