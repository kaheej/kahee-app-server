Account Deletion Guide

To delete your account from BurningON Challenge, please follow the instructions below.

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: Deleting your account will permanently remove all data and cannot be recovered.

Information Deleted with Account

  • Profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • All health records (weight, body fat, muscle mass, etc.)
  • Meal records and calorie data
  • Diet program progress
  • Challenge participation records and achievements
  • Custom foods and meal templates
  • Sleep, exercise, and water intake records
  • App settings and personalization information

üè• Health Data Notice: Deleted health records cannot be recovered. Please backup any data needed for medical purposes before deletion.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Launch App and Login

Open the BurningON Challenge app and login with the account you wish to delete.

2

Navigate to More Tab

Select the 'More' tab from the bottom navigation menu.

3

Enter Settings Menu

Select the 'Settings' menu option.

4

Select Account Management

Choose 'Account Management' or 'Profile Management' from the menu.

5

Request Account Deletion

Press the 'Delete Account' button and enter your password to verify identity before proceeding with deletion.

üí° Note: If you have ongoing diet programs or challenges, all progress will be lost upon account deletion.

Before Deleting Your Account

  • Backup important health records or meal data using screenshots or export features.
  • Cancel any active paid subscriptions first.
  • Check data backed up to Google Drive or cloud storage.
  • You cannot re-register with the same email within 30 days after deletion.

How to Backup Your Data

  • Export Health Data: Settings > Data Management > Export Data
  • Save Meal Records: Save as PDF using the share button on each meal screen
  • Save Program Results: Download the report provided upon program completion

Data Processing Policy

Upon account deletion request:

  • All personal data is immediately deleted.
  • Backup server data is completely removed within 30 days.
  • Anonymized statistical data may be retained for service improvement.

Account Recovery

Within 7 days after account deletion, you can request recovery through customer service. However, some data may not be recoverable.
